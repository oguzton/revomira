1. Introduction

At Revomira, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service. It also details how we work with third-party services to provide our platform functionality.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, organization details, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide during registration.

Review Data

When you connect your business accounts to review platforms, we collect and store review data including review text, ratings, reviewer information (as provided by the platform), dates, and platform-specific metadata. This data is necessary to provide our review management and analysis services.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our service, including pages visited, features used, and actions taken. This helps us improve our platform and user experience.

Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system. This information is used for security, analytics, and service optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve our review management services, including aggregating reviews from multiple platforms, performing AI-powered analysis, and generating insights.

Communication

We use your contact information to send you technical notices, support messages, service updates, and respond to your inquiries.

Security and Fraud Prevention

We use your information to detect, prevent, and address technical issues, security threats, and fraudulent activity.

Service Improvement

We analyze usage patterns and feedback to improve our services, develop new features, and enhance user experience.

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

Platform Integrations (OAuth Services)

To provide review management services, we integrate with third-party review platforms (such as Google My Business, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, and similar services). When you connect these platforms to your account:

  • We use OAuth 2.0 authentication protocols to securely access these platforms on your behalf.
  • We do NOT store your login credentials. We only request and store encrypted OAuth access tokens.
  • These encrypted access tokens allow us to retrieve only your review data. We have no direct access to your account or other data.
  • Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share them with third parties.
  • You can disconnect any platform integration at any time via account settings.
  • The review data we retrieve is stored securely in our systems and receives the same privacy protections as other data we collect.

AI Analysis Services

We use third-party AI services to analyze your reviews and provide insights such as sentiment analysis, topic detection, and response suggestions. When you use our AI analysis features:

  • Review text content is sent to AI providers with strong data protection practices.
  • We only send the text necessary for analysis, and no extra personally identifiable information.
  • AI providers process this data according to their privacy policies and are obligated to use it only for our analysis requests.
  • Providers do not retain your review data for training or any other purpose.
  • We may use multiple AI providers for reliability, meaning your data may be processed by different providers.
  • You control whether reviews are analyzed by enabling or disabling AI features in your settings.

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand usage and improve our platform. These services:

  • Collect anonymized usage data such as page views, feature usage, and behavior patterns.
  • Do not collect personally identifiable information without your explicit consent.
  • Can be controlled through your cookie preferences.
  • Help us improve user experience and service performance.

Data Processing Agreements

All third-party providers we work with must:

  • Comply with applicable data protection laws.
  • Implement appropriate security measures.
  • Use your data only for the specific services we contract.
  • Not share your data without authorization.
  • Delete or return your data when our agreement ends.

5. Data Security

Technical Safeguards

We implement security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication protocols, regular security audits, and access controls.

Organizational Safeguards

Access to your information is limited to personnel who need it to provide our services, and all are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal requirements. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal information per our retention policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You can access, update, and correct your personal information through account settings or by contacting us.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.

Data Portability

You can export your review data and account information in a machine-readable format.

Opt-Out Rights

You can opt out of analytics, marketing communications, and other non-essential processing.

Platform Disconnection

You can disconnect any third-party platform at any time, revoking our access immediately.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to track activity, store preferences, and hold certain information. Essential cookies are required for the service to function. Analytics and marketing cookies require your consent and can be managed through your cookie preferences. See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data regardless of where it is processed.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately.
